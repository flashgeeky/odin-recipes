Banana Bread

About the Recipe

The banana bread recipe creates the most delicious, moist loaf filled with heavenly banana flavor

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Brown Sugar
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a pan and bake in a preheated oven.
