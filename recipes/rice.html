Rice

How to Cook Rice

Rice is a staple food and contains mostly carbohydrates. We will walk you through the process of preparing your own rice.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Salt
  • Water

The Steps

  1. Measure the rice you want to cook in a bowl.
  2. Rinse the rice to remove excess starch
  3. Use the right amount of water.
  4. Add salt
  5. Bring the water to a boil.
  6. Maintain a simmer.
  7. Cook without stirring
  8. Drain when the rice is softened
