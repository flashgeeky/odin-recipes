Chocolate Chip Pancake Recipe

About Chocolate chip pancake

Who doesn't like a light, delicious and fluffy chocolate pancake? Learn the best way to prepare yours at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips, or more to taste
  • Cooking spray
