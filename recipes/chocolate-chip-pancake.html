Chocolate Chip Pancake Recipe

About Chocolate chip pancake

Who doesn't like a light, delicious and fluffy chocolate pancake? Learn the best way to prepare yours at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips, or more to taste
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Combine milk and vinegar in a bowl and allow for 5 minutes to sour.
  2. Combine baking soda, sugar, flour, baking powder, and salt in a larg mixing bowl.
  3. Whisk egg, butter, and vanilla extract into buttermilk mixture until well combined; add flour mixture and whisk until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips. The pancake batter will be thick. Allow batter to rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Heat a large skillet over medium-low heat, and coat with cooking spray.
  5. Pour about Â¼ cup of batter for each pancake onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, 2 to 4 minutes. Flip pancake with a spatula, and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 more minutes. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve immediately.
